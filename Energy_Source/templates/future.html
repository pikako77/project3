US Energy Consumption in 2017




  • Global coal production is likely to remain steady thru 2040
  • The top three coal-consuming countries are China, the US and India, which will account for around 70% of coal thru 2040.
  • Natural gas will account for the largest increase in world primary energy consumption after renewables.
  • Even though the use of renewables is expected to grow faster than fossil fuels, the US Energy Information Administration (EIA) says coal, oil and natural gas will still account for 77% of our energy in 2040.
